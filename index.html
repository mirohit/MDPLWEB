Frequently asked
How do I request access to a dashboard?
Raise a request via the Service Portal and select "Dashboard Access". Our team reviews all requests within one business day.
A dashboard is not loading — what do I do?
Try refreshing your browser or clearing cache. If the issue persists, email us with the dashboard name and a screenshot of any error message.
How often are dashboards refreshed?
Most dashboards refresh daily at 6:00 AM IST. The "Updated" date on each card shows the last confirmed data refresh.
Can I request a new or custom dashboard?
Yes — email us with your requirements and data sources. We'll respond within 3 business days.
